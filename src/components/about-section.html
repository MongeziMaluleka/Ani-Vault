About AniList Vault

What is AniList Vault?

AniList Vault is a comprehensive anime and manga tracking platform that helps you organize your watching experience. Keep track of what you're currently watching, what you've completed, and what you plan to watch in the future.

Our platform uses the Jikan API to provide you with up-to-date information about thousands of anime and manga titles.

Features

  • Search for anime or manga using the Jikan API
  • Add titles to your Watching, Completed, or Plan to Watch lists
  • View detailed information about each title
  • Get recommendations for streaming platforms
  • Track your progress through series
  • Netflix-style user interface for easy browsing
